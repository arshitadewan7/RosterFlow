RosterFlow is a lightweight, offline-ready work-hour and shift manager built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It helps you track shifts, calculate income, monitor work restrictions (like 24 hours/week or 48 hours/fortnight), and detect roster conflicts across multiple workplaces.
